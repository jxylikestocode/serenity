# Serenity — Project Brief

## Strategy & Scope

### Problem Statement

Mental health is one of the most pressing issues facing post-secondary students today. According to the Canadian Alliance of Student Associations, 68% of students report feeling overwhelmed, and over 50% experience anxiety severe enough to impact their academic performance. Despite this, barriers to seeking help remain high:

- **Wait times:** Campus counselling waitlists can stretch weeks or months
- **Cost:** Private therapy averages $150–250/session, prohibitive for students
- **Stigma:** Many students avoid seeking help due to fear of judgment
- **Accessibility:** Traditional in-person services have limited hours and availability

### Target Audience

- Post-secondary students aged 18–25
- Primary focus: Sheridan College students
- Secondary: Any young adult seeking accessible mental wellness tools
- Characteristics: Digital-native, mobile-first, privacy-conscious

### Product Vision

**Serenity** is a free, anonymous web application that provides students with accessible mental wellness tools. It is **not** therapy and does not replace professional care — instead, it offers daily self-care habits, coping exercises, and peer support to help students manage stress, track their emotional well-being, and access crisis resources when needed.

### Core Features

| Feature | Description | Purpose |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Mood Tracking | Daily emoji-based check-ins with optional notes | Build self-awareness of emotional patterns |
| Mood History | Visual timeline (Chart.js) of past moods | Identify trends and triggers |
| Journal | Private, full CRUD journaling space | Process thoughts and emotions through writing |
| Coping Tools | Interactive exercises (breathing, grounding, prompts, affirmations) | Evidence-based stress and anxiety relief |
| Community | Anonymous peer support vent space | Reduce isolation, give and receive support |
| Crisis Detection | Keyword scanning with resource surfacing | Connect at-risk users to real help immediately |

### What Serenity Is NOT

- Not a replacement for professional therapy or counselling
- Not a diagnostic tool — it does not assess or treat conditions
- Not a social media platform — it's a wellness tool
- Not collecting personal data — fully anonymous by design

### Competitive Landscape

| App | Differentiator | Serenity Advantage |
|-----|---------------|-------------------|
| Headspace | Subscription-based, meditation focus | Free, student-focused, peer support |
| Calm | Premium pricing, sleep-focused | Free, anonymous, crisis detection |
| 7 Cups | Requires account, volunteer listeners | No account needed, fully anonymous |
| Woebot | AI chatbot, clinical approach | Lighter touch, community-driven |

### Success Metrics

- User engagement: daily check-in streaks, journal entries created
- Community health: posts created, replies, support counts
- Crisis intervention: crisis resources shown when needed
- Accessibility: WCAG AA compliance, mobile responsive

### Scope Boundaries

**In Scope:** All features listed above, responsive web app, MongoDB persistence, anonymous sessions, deployment to Render.com

**Out of Scope:** Native mobile apps, AI chatbot therapy, user accounts/authentication, paid features, data analytics dashboard
